Club FAQ

Everything you need to know about joining.

Not at all! Innovation requires diverse perspectives. We need designers, coders, writers, and business minds just as much as we need biologists and physicists. "Science for All" means everyone.

We have agreements with specific partner companies who reserve internship slots for our club members. You apply through our internal portal, and our club leadership provides a recommendation letter to the employer.

Membership is free for all university students. Some specific workshops or hackathons might have a small entry fee to cover food and materials, but general access is open to all.
