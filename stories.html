"I didn't have any experience on my resume. I joined the Innovation Club, built a water-quality sensor for a semester project, and used that exact project to pass my technical interview."

Sarah joined the club as a sophomore majoring in Biology. Feeling disconnected from the tech side of science, she joined a club team building IoT devices.

Through the club's Direct Internship Pathway, her resume was flagged to recruiters at BioGen, leading to a summer internship and eventually a full-time role.

How We Build Leaders

The typical path of a club member.

Join & Explore

Attend general meetings, meet mentors, and find a team that interests you.

Build

Spend the semester building a project. Fail fast, learn hard skills, and prototype.

Showcase

Present at the Science Fair. Add the project to your resume and portfolio.

Intern

Use club connections to land an internship. 85% of our project leads get placed.
